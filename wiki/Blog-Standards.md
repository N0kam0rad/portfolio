# Blog Standards

Build logs should show how the work was made. A good post is not just a launch note. It is a tiny case study.

## Every blog post should include

1. A clear hook
2. The problem or creative constraint
3. Screenshots or process visuals
4. The workflow used
5. What AI did
6. What human judgment did
7. What broke or drifted
8. How it was fixed
9. What changed in the product
10. What would be built next

## Screenshot requirements

Every build-log post should include relevant screenshots.

Use screenshots to show:

- Before / after states
- UI flows
- Prompt outputs
- Reference images
- Production boards
- Error or drift examples
- Final shipped state

Do not rely on text only when the post is about visual or product work.

## Recommended post structure

```markdown
# Title

## The quick version

One paragraph explaining what was built and why it matters.

## The problem

What was hard, annoying, expensive, repetitive, or creatively blocked?

## The workflow

Step-by-step explanation of how the thing was built.

## Screenshots

Show the actual product, board, reference pack, or visual output.

## What AI helped with

Be specific. Prompting, code scaffolding, visual exploration, debugging, naming, QA, etc.

## What I had to direct

Taste, product judgment, privacy, UX, editing, final copy, and what to reject.

## What broke

Drift, bugs, layout issues, bad copy, false confidence, wrong assets, etc.

## What I learned

Make the post useful to someone else trying to build with AI.

## What I would build next

End with momentum.
```

## Screenshot captions

Captions should explain why the screenshot matters.

Good:

> The final consultation board became the card thumbnail because it shows the core value in one frame: facial mapping, analysis, and treatment planning.

Weak:

> Screenshot of app.

## Voice

Write like a builder explaining the recipe, not a marketer fogging the room.

Use:

- Specific nouns
- Real tradeoffs
- Clear steps
- Honest lessons
- Warm confidence

Avoid:

- Generic AI hype
- Fake certainty
- Empty founder-speak
- Posting screenshots without context

## Blog QA checklist

- [ ] Includes screenshots or process visuals
- [ ] Explains how it was made
- [ ] Explains what AI helped with
- [ ] Explains what human judgment controlled
- [ ] Includes at least one concrete lesson
- [ ] Has privacy-safe images and copy
- [ ] Links to the related spec or demo

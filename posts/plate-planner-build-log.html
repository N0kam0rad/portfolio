How I built My Plate Planner

A meal-planning concept that uses privacy-safe dummy data and focuses on realistic planning instead of diet-app punishment.

The spark

I wanted planning that could handle preferences, protein goals, groceries, and real-life appetite without becoming another obsessive tracker.

How I built it

I broke the app into meal slots, grocery logic, ingredient swaps, and simple visual planning. AI helped structure the flow and keep the UI from becoming a spreadsheet.

What is next

Build a usable dummy-data version and add privacy-safe screenshots before calling it Served.

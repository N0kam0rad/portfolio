How I built Roommate Tracker

A privacy-safe shared-rent tracker concept for dummy-data demos, not real household finances.

RENT
TRACKER

The spark

Shared bills create tiny recurring conflicts. I wanted a simple way to show who owes what without needing a spreadsheet or a group chat argument.

How I built it

I framed the app around balances, rent split, payment history, and dummy data. The rule is simple: show the workflow without exposing real people or real numbers.

What is next

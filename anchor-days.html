How it works

One small mystery, every single day.

The puzzle reveal is the hook — kids don't finish their routine for a chart, they finish it to see what's underneath.

1

Build the routine

Caregivers set up morning & bedtime steps once — visual, simple, no reading required for younger kids.

2

3

Questions

Before you join

Is this a banking app, like Greenlight?

No. Anchor Days doesn't move real money or link to a bank account. It tracks routines and points, and caregivers hand out real-world rewards themselves based on what the app tracks — no debit card, no money-transmission concerns.

Is it specifically for autism or ADHD?

It's built with those families in mind — clear visual steps, low-overwhelm celebrations, and a caregiver layer that keeps routines consistent — but any kid who struggles with follow-through can use it.

What devices will it run on?

iOS and Android, planned as a real installable app (not just a website) from day one.

What it is

A two-sided prototype with a guided client side and an operator side, built to show the full concept before investing in a production backend.

Why I built it

The original idea lived in my head as a high-trust service workflow. I wanted to make it visible, clickable, and easy to pitch.

How I built it

I started with the data model and states first, then used AI to scaffold the interface, tighten the flow, and polish the story of the demo.

What changed

The first pass felt too generic. The stronger version needed clearer hierarchy, trust language, and a more memorable board-style interface.

Next step

Validate the workflow with real service providers, then decide what belongs in a production build versus what should stay as demo-only storytelling.
